About Me

My name is Blake and I've had a passion for technology for as long as I can remember. I may only have a year of formal programming experience, but I've been tinkering with technology my entire life. My goal is to create websites and apps that are not only visually appealing but also easy to use. From fixing old video game consoles to creating digital art and building PCs, I love exploring different aspects of technology and always looking to learn something new. When I'm not programming, you can usually find me hiking in the mountains, experimenting in the kitchen, or tending to my collection of bonsai trees. I'm excited to continue learning and growing in the tech industry.
